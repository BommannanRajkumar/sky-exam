Launch SEB Browser

Instructions

  1. Click the “Launch SEB Client” button.
  2. A file named exam-session.seb (or similar) will be downloaded to your computer.
  3. Locate and double‑click the downloaded .seb file—this will open Safe Exam Browser (SEB) and start the exam session.
  4. Complete your exam inside SEB.
  5. Once you finish, click on the “Finish Exam / Session” button inside SEB to close it and inform the examiner that you've completed the exam.
